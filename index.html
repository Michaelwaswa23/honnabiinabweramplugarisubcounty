Lugari Constituency

Transforming Lugari, One Ward at a Time

Lugari Constituency, founded in 1988, is located in Kakamega County and home to over 167,000 residents. It spans 367 km² and includes eight wards like Chekalini, Kongoni, Lumakanda, and Mautuma. Known for its agricultural strength and community-driven growth, Lugari is on a steady path toward inclusive development.

Representing this vibrant region is Hon. Nabii Nabwera, a visionary leader elected in 2022 under the Orange Democratic Movement (ODM). With a career rooted in human resources, governance, and servant leadership, he brings experience and empathy to the heart of public service.

Through educational reforms, ICT transformation, rural electrification, clean water access, and modern infrastructure, Hon. Nabwera is reshaping Lugari into a smart, self-sufficient constituency. This website is your digital doorway to that journey—built on transparency, engagement, and people-first leadership.
