Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To build a united, educated, empowered, and prosperous Lugari Constituency where every citizen has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to community development.

Our Mission

To serve with integrity, inclusivity, and transparency by implementing development projects, empowering youth and women, investing in education and agriculture, and enhancing accountability through people-driven leadership.

Core Values

Transparency

Open and accountable leadership with public access to information and progress.

Inclusivity

Ensure every ward, group, and citizen is considered and served equally.

Empowerment

Support youth, women, farmers, and small businesses to uplift the local economy.

Education

Promote quality, accessible education through modern infrastructure and bursaries.

Innovation

Use digital tools, ICT centers, and tech-based solutions for a smarter constituency.

Unity & Peace

Foster peaceful coexistence and dialogue across all communities in Lugari.

About Lugari

Lugari is one of Kakamega’s fastest-growing constituencies, driven by innovation, youth, agriculture, and community spirit.
