About Hon. Nabii Nabwera

❮ ❯

Early Life & Education

Born in Mugunga village to the late Daraja Nabwera and Julia Matakala, Hon. Nabii Nabwera was shaped by stories of civic duty and community values. He is a proud member of the Tachoni sub-tribe of the Luhya community.

  • 1974–1981: Mugunga Primary School
  • 1982–1985: Kivaywa Secondary School
  • 1986–1987: Musingu High School
  • 1988–1992: Moi University – Bachelor of Arts (2nd Class Upper)
  • 1996–1997: Postgraduate Diploma – Human Resource Management (TUK)
  • ILO Centre, Geneva – Certificate in Organisation Development & Change Management

Professional Career

  • KBC – Personnel Manager (1992–1999)
  • South Nyanza Sugar – Employee Relations Manager
  • Coca-Cola Rift Valley – HR Manager
  • Del Monte – HR Lead
  • Insteel Ltd – HR Consultant
  • Kenya Bureau of Standards – Consultant
  • Kakamega County – Chief of Staff & Advisor
  • Council of Governors – Head of Liaison Services
  • Chief Officer – Livestock & Veterinary Services, Kakamega

Political Journey

Inspired by Mzee Burudi Nabwera (Kenya’s first ambassador to the US), Hon. Nabii joined ODM and contested Parliament four times. After years of mentorship, grassroots engagement, and direct development projects, he won the Lugari seat in 2022 with 23,159 votes.

Community Leadership & Impact

  • Chairs barazas across all wards for citizen feedback
  • Mentors youth, sponsors students, and builds modern schools
  • Drives agricultural, education, digital, and sports development
  • Member of Parliament Committees: Appropriations, Agriculture, Education, Social Services

“Leadership thrives on listening. When we lift each other, we all rise.”

About Lugari

Lugari is one of Kakamega’s fastest-growing constituencies, driven by innovation, youth, agriculture, and community spirit.
